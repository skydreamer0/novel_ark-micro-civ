# Three Pressure Factions Design

**Date:** 2026-03-12

**Goal:** Extend the mothership setting with three new factions that create ongoing, escalating pressure on the protagonist through finance, transit control, and ideology.

---

## 1. Approved Direction

The expansion adds three linked factions instead of one:

- **清譜會**: a semi-official pollution-finance network that controls contamination ratings, purification priority, and seal-down asset liquidation.
- **盲舵會**: a black-route pilot guild that monopolizes obsolete corridors, expired access codes, and hull-cutting crews for downgraded sectors and dead sectors.
- **續焰團**: a recruitment-and-mobilization group that uses Ever-Sailing rhetoric to package exploitation as sacrifice, redemption, and upward mobility.

These factions are designed as one harvesting chain rather than three isolated villains:

1. **續焰團** persuades people to step into dangerous work.
2. **盲舵會** sells the route into sealed or downgraded zones.
3. **清譜會** decides whether the team, cargo, and contamination are worth saving on the way back out.

---

## 2. Narrative Function

### 清譜會

- Adds a new institutional choke point after extraction but before profit.
- Makes pollution, deposits, and purification timing into weaponized economic pressure.
- Lets the setting punish success without relying only on direct violence.

### 盲舵會

- Creates a reusable high-risk collaborator for dead-sector and blockade stories.
- Enables betrayal-heavy exploration arcs without duplicating Gray Chain's civilian logistics role.
- Gives sealed corridors, obsolete maps, and decompression timing a monetized black market.

### 續焰團

- Occupies the bottom-up hope market before the protagonist can.
- Turns Ever-Sailing ideology into a practical recruitment machine instead of only a broadcast narrative.
- Creates social conflict where people walk into the grinder "voluntarily."

---

## 3. Setting Fit Checks

### 熵潮與規則密度

The new factions do not solve entropy. They are all adaptive parasites inside an already collapsing system:

- **清譜會** rationing purification reflects finite pollution-processing capacity.
- **盲舵會** profits because official corridors are sealed as sectors degrade.
- **續焰團** thrives because people need meaning while the ship continues to consume lives.

### 母艦三條硬約束

- **能源**: none of the factions create energy or rule density; they only redistribute survival access.
- **人口**: 續焰團 and 盲舵會 both help move expendable labor into lethal zones, reinforcing the ship's disposable labor logic.
- **污染**: 清譜會 makes the limited purification ceiling visible and cruel by turning cleanup priority into a market.

### 主角核心差異

The expansion does not dilute the protagonist's uniqueness:

- The factions optimize extraction and sacrifice.
- The protagonist remains the only path toward a genuine anti-entropy alternative.
- Their cruelty sharpens the contrast between "who gets consumed next" and "how to stop needing consumption at all."

## 5. Writing Notes

- 清譜會 should feel clinical, procedural, and bloodless.
- 盲舵會 should feel pragmatic, secretive, and betrayal-prone.
- 續焰團 should feel warm on the surface and predatory underneath.
- The three factions should be reusable across multiple arcs as rotating pressure sources, not one-off antagonists.
